Hi, I'm Rachel

Holistic Wellness Guide · RYT-200

I’m a yoga teacher with down-to-earth pragmatism and a soft spot for tender seasons. I came to this work through my own healing and by helping hold down the fort during a family deployment. The throughline: tiny, kind practices beat heroic plans every time.

My approach

  • Gentle & grounded: slow shapes, breath, pauses—plus 5–10 minute practices you can actually keep.
  • Consent & choice: invitational language and go-by-feel pacing. You’re in charge of your body.
  • Skills, not slogans: grounding, purposeful regulation, mental agility, strengths on purpose, and connection.

What a session feels like

  • Arrive: take 3 easy breaths; let your exhale be a touch longer than your inhale.
  • Warm & move: gentle, supportive shapes with options for every body.
  • Regulate: one simple tool (e.g., orienting, lengthened exhale, or a sensory reset).
  • Tend the spark: a tiny take-home you can do in 3–5 minutes this week.
  • Close: rest or a brief reflection—tea timer optional.
